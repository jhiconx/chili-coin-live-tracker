Important Data Notice

Custody classification: “Non-custodial” and “custodial” reflect the Chili Coin operating model supplied for this tracker. A blockchain explorer shows public addresses and activity, but it does not independently prove who controls private keys.

Holder totals: The combined chain total adds the two chain-level figures. It is not a verified count of unique people, because one person or address may be represented more than once.

Refresh behavior: The tracker is near-real-time, polling public explorer/indexer data every 20 seconds. Explorer indexing can lag the latest Base block.
